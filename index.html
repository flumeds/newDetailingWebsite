AUTO DETAILING FAQ

What is included in a full auto detail?

A full auto detail typically includes exterior washing, interior car cleaning, wheel cleaning, glass cleaning, and finishing work for tires and trim.

Do you offer mobile detailing?

Yes. Sleek Detailing offers mobile detailing appointments for customers who want service at a convenient location in the local service area.

How often should a car be detailed?

Many drivers schedule detailing every few months, but the right timing depends on mileage, parking conditions, weather exposure, and how often the vehicle is used.
